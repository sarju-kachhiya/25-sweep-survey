1 - How do I participate in Surveys?

After Registration, members are provided with the list of Surveys available. Upon completion of initial screening, Members will be redirected to the client's site and participate in surveys. Also when a client contacts us with a survey, we then send invitations out to members who we believe MAY qualify for that study. The e-mail will contain a link to the survey and a login code. The first part of the survey traditionally contains several screening questions. If you pass the screening process, as determined by the client, you will then continue on with the survey and be paid.

2 - How long do surveys take to complete?

Approximately 10-15 minutes.

3 - How do I get Paid?

Our Minumum payment amount is $20, You need to redeem your payment in dollar amount. Once your ADS Points reach to 2000 you are free to redeem your points into equivalent dollar amount. your payment will be sent to your paypal account with 15 to 20 working after your request. our support team will contact you to verify your account. This helps us to keep fraud related activity in check. Once it has been verified, your payment request will be submitted, and all payments are made directly to your PayPal account if paypal email is verified.(except weekends or holidays)

4 - How are payments made?

ADS process 100% payments by PayPal, it is the most easy and convenient way to send and receive money online. You can Register with paypal by going to www.paypal.com. survezip will make payment to users having US verified primary PayPal account only. For more information, please visit Paypal.com

5 - How much will I be paid?

Our Minumum payment amount is $20 (2000 ADS Points), You have to request for payment here, Once your ADS Points reach to 2000, your payment will be sent to your paypal account within 15 to 20 working days after your redeem request.

6 - When do I get paid?

Your payment will be sent to your paypal account within 15 to 20 working days after your redeem request. .

7 - Why do you ask personal information when I register?

We ask demographic questions such as age, gender, marital status to ensure we have a diverse, representative membership database. We only use this information to group our respondents for inclusion in appropriate surveys

8 - How do I unsubscribe?

If you wish to unsubscribe, please go to Unsubscribe Link, which will remove your data from our database.

9 - How long are your surveys usually posted?

Our surveys are generally posted for two weeks, but the life span of a survey varies. We have some that are posted for a few days and some that are posted for 24 hours. If there is a tremendous response to a particular survey, it may be closed in a day. Once we have collected the data needed by the client, we are required to close the survey. We will always tell you the time requirements when we invite you to participate in a survey.

10 - Do members get paid for referring ADS to friends?

Yes! Refer your friends to ADS and earn 15% of their survey earnings till the life of their account. For example, You refer Bob to ADS, Bob becomes a member and completes online surveys and evaluations, once he requests payment for $100 from ADS, $15 is immediately credited in your account.

When you successfully complete the surveys and get approved for reward, you will be eligible for payment. It will take minimum of two working days and up to a maximum of 1 week to get your offers approved from the advertiser.
