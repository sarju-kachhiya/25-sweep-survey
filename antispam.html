Antispam Policy

  • Home
  • antispam

You may not promote your referral links through unsolicited emailing (i.e. SPAMMING), newsgroup postings, or any other method of mass communication. Failure to comply will result in immediate termination of your membership with alldaysurvey.com and may result in legal prosecution.

ADS strictly enforces anti-spamming laws. Spamming is an internationally monitored crime that has severe criminal and civil penalties. Any member caught spamming will not only have their account terminated immediately and lose any past, present and future earnings, but shall also be held liable for Spamming as we shall cooperate with any authorities and investigations that may arise from the Spamming incident. ADS may charge a fee per email sent. Multiple signups shall be grounds for immediate termination of all involved accounts. Signing up multiple times from the same computer also constitutes fraud.

ADS will file charges for recovery of any earnings received from multiple signups.
